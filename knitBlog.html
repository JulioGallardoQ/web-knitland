Winter is coming to knitland!

Are you a fan of Game of Thrones, the epic fantasy series based on the novels by George R. R. Martin? Do you love the intricate costumes, the stunning landscapes, and the fierce dragons? If so, you might be interested in knitting some items inspired by the show, whether for yourself or as a gift for a fellow fan. In this blog post, I will share with you some of the best Game of Thrones knitting patterns that you can find online, from scarves and hats to blankets and socks. These patterns are suitable for different skill levels and yarn weights, and they feature motifs and symbols from the show, such as the house sigils, the dragon scales, and the famous phrases. So grab your needles and yarn, and let’s get started!

One of the most iconic elements of Game of Thrones is the house sigils, the emblems that represent the different noble families of Westeros. Each sigil has a unique animal and a motto, such as the direwolf and “Winter is Coming” for House Stark, or the dragon and “Fire and Blood” for House Targaryen.
